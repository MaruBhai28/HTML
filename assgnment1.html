Peacock

The peacock is the national bird of India and national pride for all of its citizens. Peacocks mainly thrive by eating plants and small animals and are one of the largest birds in the country. You can find peacocks all over the country. They can be seen pecking the ground for food and on the tall trees in the forest.

Lion

Lions are large cats with muscular bodies, short heads, and round ears. They have yellow-gold coats, and adult males have manes that can be blond, reddish-brown, or black.
